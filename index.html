Who we are

Crowdquest is a platform and community for virtual assistants who offer research and writing services. We are a team of freelance and contract virtual assistants that saw a need for a supportive community, so we built one!

At Crowdquest, you can network with other community members, get helpful tips and strategies to become better at your work, learn how to determine your rates, prepare for taxes, and more.

Crowdquest is built for freelancers working globally and remotely. Our team is located all around the world to provide you with the support you need to succeed in your local market.
